Let's build your course media, together.

Thinking about videos, an online course, or something immersive? Here's the honest picture of what partnering with ODL involves, and how much of your time it really takes.

Your time commitment
A few hours a week.
ODL's team takes on the heavy lifting: design, filming, editing, and project management. You focus on the ideas and expertise, and stay as hands-on as you'd like. In a typical week that looks like:

Some weeks are heavier (filming and reviewing your videos take a bit more), and many weeks are lighter. Work spreads comfortably across the project, which usually runs over a semester or two for a course, or a few weeks to a couple of months for a smaller piece. These are ODL's planning estimates; we'll pin down the real numbers for your project together.

The whole process, in plain terms

What you actually do

Three simple phases. Bring what you already have, do as much of the making as you'd like, and ODL supports the rest.

1

Shape it

Share your vision and learning goals. We sit down with you and turn them into a course map, the one-page blueprint everyone builds from.

A few hours, up front
2

Create it

You provide the content, in whatever form your project needs. If it includes video, you step in front of the camera; roughly ~1 hour to script and ~1 hour to film per video, and we handle everything around it. Many smaller projects, like a single module or an AI tool, involve little or no filming at all.

Spread across a few sessions
3

Review & launch

Watch the drafts, tell us what to tweak, and approve the final. We build it in Canvas, make it accessible, and hand you a finished course.

Short check-ins

What ODL can handle for you

You bring the expertise. We can take all of this off your plate, or share it if you'd like to stay hands-on:

✓ Instructional & learning design
✓ Filming, studio & video editing
✓ Graphics & animation
✓ Accessibility & captioning
✓ Canvas / LMS setup
✓ Project management & scheduling
